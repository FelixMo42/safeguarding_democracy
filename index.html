The Initiative for Safeguarding Democracy

Democracy is under threat. Across the world, authoritarian movements are on the rise. From the AfD in Germany, to Trump in the U.S., Orbán in Hungary, Erdoğan in Turkey, and Modi in India, we are witnessing a global trend of democratic backsliding, the erosion of the rule of law, and increasing restrictions on civil liberties. Democracy is more than just a political system; it is the foundation of a good life. That’s why we must stand together to protect it.

We have founded Safeguarding Democracy to bring together activists and interested people who are committed to defending democracy and opposing authoritarianism – in Germany, the EU, and around the world. Our goal is to educate, learn together, raise awareness, and support one another in taking concrete actions to protect democracy. To achieve this, we have set up a chat group and hold monthly meetings.

Would you like to get involved? If so, please email us at safeguarding-democracy@posteo.de



Our Common Purpose

No democracy is perfect, and yet the liberal democracy that we have enjoyed has contributed greatly to peace, prosperity and the increase of civil rights. Despite its successes and maybe because of them, liberal democracy has come under assault, both from within and from without.

We are a group of people who share the common goal to protect, preserve, and promote liberal democracy.

To this end, we are dedicated to activating, mobilizing, connecting, and educating activists and individuals committed to defending liberal democracy.

Our Political Values

In this context, a democracy is a political system in which those who govern are elected by the people in free and fair multiparty elections.

A liberal democracy also guarantees the rule of law, separation of powers, and individual rights.

As liberal democrats, we believe in the equality of all people regardless of their identity. We condemn discrimination on the basis of race, gender, religion, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity and other identity features.

Since we value pluralism of opinion, people of every political orientation are welcome, as long as they are committed to our shared goal to safeguard democracy.

Apart from this, we pursue no other political agenda.

Our Approach To Dialogue

We do not view political disagreements as a problem to be solved, but as an epistemic strength and inherent feature of democracy.

In this light, we consider respectful debate to be a cornerstone of informed decision-making and problem-solving. It is through the sharing of different perspectives that we can create a stronger, more resilient democracy.

We embrace the Scout Mindset, which encourages us to approach differing views with the goal of understanding and learning rather than merely defending our own. We acknowledge that our own beliefs may evolve in response to newinformation, and we remain open to revising our views in pursuit of truth.

While we recognize that emotionalization may sometimes be necessary in democratic politics, we reject it in our internal exchanges. We hold ourselves accountable to the values of openness, curiosity, and humility, and strive to foster an environment where every voice is heard, valued, and respected.
